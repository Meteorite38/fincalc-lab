How to use these fairly

Two rules make either/or comparisons honest. First, hold total cash committed equal — if one path costs less per month, the fair comparison invests the difference rather than quietly spending it. Second, compare at the same end date — a 15-year mortgage vs a 30-year only makes sense measured over the same 30 years. Every tool above follows both rules, and each page explains the assumptions and the math it uses, so you can disagree with an input and change it.
