FinCalc Lab exists because most financial calculators online are either advertising funnels for loans, cluttered beyond usability, or black boxes that hand you a number without showing the work.

We build the opposite: fast, free calculators that show the formula, explain the assumptions, and walk through a worked example in plain English — so you leave not just with a number, but with an understanding of how the number was made.

## Who runs this site

FinCalc Lab is built and maintained by a graduate researcher in accounting and financial data analysis, with a background spanning corporate finance, financial statement analysis and quantitative modeling. Every calculator's logic and every article is reviewed against standard financial formulas and reputable sources before publication.

## Our principles

1. **Privacy by default.** Every calculator runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. The numbers you type — salaries, debts, savings — are never transmitted, stored, or seen by us.
2. **Show the math.** Each tool documents its formula and assumptions. If a result surprises you, you can trace exactly why.
3. **Education, not advice.** We explain how financial mathematics works. We do not know your situation, and nothing here is a substitute for a qualified adviser, accountant or tax professional.
4. **Free, supported by ads.** The site is funded by unobtrusive advertising, which lets us keep every tool free with no accounts and no paywalls.

## Corrections

Accuracy matters more than pride. If you believe a calculator or article contains an error, please [contact us](/contact/) — verified corrections are published promptly.
