*Last updated: July 2026*

This policy explains what information FinCalc Lab ("we", "this site") collects when you visit, and how it is used.

## The short version

- Everything you type into our calculators stays in your browser. We never receive it.
- We do not require accounts and do not collect names, emails or financial details.
- Like most websites, we use advertising and basic analytics, which rely on cookies you can control.

## Calculator inputs

All calculators on this site run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. The values you enter — incomes, debts, savings, prices — are processed on your device and are **never transmitted to, stored on, or visible to our servers**.

## Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate traffic: which pages are visited, from which country, on what kind of device. This data is aggregated and cannot be used by us to identify you personally.

## Advertising and cookies

This site is supported by advertising, including Google AdSense.

- Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites.
- Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
- You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/). You can also opt out of some third-party cookies via [www.aboutads.info](https://www.aboutads.info/choices/).

Depending on your region (for example the EEA or UK), you may be shown a consent banner allowing you to accept or reject non-essential cookies before they are set.

## Data sharing

We do not sell, rent or trade any personal information. We have no personal information to sell.

## External links

Articles may link to external sites we consider reputable. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

## Children

This site provides general financial education and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy as the site or regulations evolve. The date at the top reflects the latest revision.
